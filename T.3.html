Hacker ^^


A hacker is any skilled computer expert that uses their technical knowledge to overcome a problem.
While "hacker" can refer to any computer programmer,
the term has become associated in popular culture with a "security hacker",
someone who, with their technical knowledge,
uses bugs or exploits to break into computer systems.
Hackers are people involved with circumvention of computer security.
